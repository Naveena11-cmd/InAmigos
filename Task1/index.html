Who We Are

A Foundation Rooted in
Community & Purpose

InAmigos Foundation was established in 2020 by Govind Shukla with a singular vision: to bridge the gaps that prevent millions of Indians from accessing education, opportunities, and a dignified life. Registered as a Section 8 (Not-for-Profit) company, InAmigos operates at the intersection of social equity and sustainable development.

From digital classrooms in under-resourced villages to plantation drives protecting local ecosystems, every initiative under InAmigos is designed to create measurable, community-led impact. We believe that true development must be holistic — educating the young, empowering women, protecting nature, and nurturing animals alongside people.

Our Flagship Initiatives

Six Projects, One Mission

Each initiative targets a specific social challenge — together, they form a comprehensive framework for community transformation.

Education 🎒

Project Bachpanshala

Bringing joyful learning to children in underserved communities through primary education, interactive digital literacy workshops, and access to tablets and reading resources — because every child deserves a bright start.

Learn more →
Empowerment 👩‍💼

Project Udaan

Equipping women with vocational skills, financial literacy, and entrepreneurship training — fostering economic independence and leadership so that women can soar beyond social barriers and build thriving livelihoods.

Learn more →
Environment 🌿

Project Prakriti

Healing India's green cover one sapling at a time — organising mass plantation drives, environmental awareness campaigns, and community clean-up missions to protect our natural ecosystems for future generations.

Learn more →
Food Security 🍱

Project Seva

Serving humanity at its most basic level — distributing nutritious meals, essential groceries, and emergency relief supplies to migrant workers, homeless individuals, and families facing food insecurity across urban and rural areas.

Learn more →
Animal Welfare 🐾

Project Jeev

Extending compassion beyond humans — feeding stray animals, supporting veterinary care camps, and advocating for the humane treatment of animals in our communities, because kindness knows no species.

Learn more →
Youth & Skills 🚀

Project Vikas

Empowering India's youth with industry-relevant skills, free virtual internships, mentorship programmes, and career-readiness workshops — building a generation of confident, capable change-makers ready for tomorrow's world.

Learn more →
